# ReturnShield Operator Playbook

This guide explains the product like you are brand-new. Follow each step in order.

---

## 1. Sign In
1. Visit `https://app.returnshield.app`.
2. Click **Create account** if you’re new, or **Log in** if you already have credentials.
3. After logging in, you will land on the **Insights Overview** dashboard.

---

## 2. Guided Walkthrough (First Login)
1. After your first sign-in every module is covered by a dark overlay.
2. Click **Continue** on the walkthrough card to reveal each section in order.
3. Read the pop-up tip that appears when a section is revealed—it explains what to look for.
4. The final step opens the **Billing** panel so you can pick a plan.

If you need to repeat the tour, use **Replay guided tour** in the top-right header at any time.

---

## 3. Dashboard Tour (Home Screen)
1. **Summary cards (top row)** — show margin saved, landfill diverted, and hours returned.
2. **Returnless intelligence table** — lists the SKUs that cost you the most. Each row explains:
   - Return volume
   - Margin at risk
   - Recommended actions (keep-it credits, donation routing, etc.)
   - *If you are still in trial mode the values are blurred. Activate billing to unblur live data.*
3. **AI Exchange Coach cards** — four action cards ranked by impact. Each card includes:
   - Headline → the move to run
   - Metrics → return volume, margin at risk, uplift
   - Checklist → follow these steps to execute
4. **VIP Resolution queue** — high-value customer tickets that need special handling.
5. **Quick start checklist** — shows Shopify connection, guided tour status, and plan activation.
6. **Resource library** — links to marketing plan, operator playbook, and billing checklist.

Every section updates automatically once data syncs.

---

## 3. Update Onboarding Stage
1. Scroll down to **Onboarding progress**.
2. Click the stage you have completed (`Connect`, `Sync`, `See insights`, `Complete`).
3. The button labeled “Current” shows where you are today.

Keeping this updated helps track rollout status with the team.

---

## 4. Generate an Exchange Playbook
1. Go to the **Exchange Automation** tab (left navigation).
2. Fill in the form:
   - Refund rate, exchange rate, AOV, logistics cost, main return reason.
3. Click **Generate playbook**.
4. The result cards tell you which policies to turn on and what ROI to expect.
5. Your latest inputs are saved locally, so the form remembers your numbers the next time you open it.
6. To wipe the form back to the defaults, click **Reset to defaults** above the submit button.

Run this every time your numbers change or before a new campaign.

---

## 5. Manage Billing
1. Open the **Billing** tab.
2. Review the status banner—it shows your current plan or trial preview.
3. Each plan card now highlights who it is ideal for and whether it’s recommended.
4. Choose a plan (Launch, Scale, Elite) and click the button.
5. Stripe checkout opens in a new tab. Complete the payment to activate the subscription.
6. After checkout you land on `/billing/success` with next steps and links to revisit key dashboards.

Need concierge support? Use the **Schedule onboarding** button in the billing footer or the marketing site.

---

## 6. Daily Workflow (10-Minute Checklist)
1. Open the dashboard.
2. Look at the **Returnless intelligence** table — action the top SKU.
3. Open the **AI Exchange Coach** card #1 — deploy the listed steps.
4. Check **VIP queue** — ensure VIP tickets are under 4 hours open.
5. Log any manual work or questions in your team’s notes.

Repeat this routine each workday. It keeps margin wins steady.
